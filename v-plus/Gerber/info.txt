       Files:
       ======
  
       All Gerber files are generated in extended gerber format (RS274X)
       *.TOP        : Film top copper
       *.BOT        :  -"- bottom copper
       *.STC        :  -"- top soldermask 
       *.STS        :  -"- bottom soldermask
       *.CRC        :  -"- cream top
       *.PLC        :  -"- silk screen top
       *.PLS        :  -"- silk screen bottom
       *.DK         : Drills plated Through (Excellon)
       *.NDK        : Drills not plated Through (Excellon)
       *.OUT        : Board outline
